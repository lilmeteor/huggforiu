About @hugforiu

I started this little project because virtual hugs helped me through some tough times, and I wanted to share that simple kindness. Now, I get to send hundreds of hug tweets daily, hoping to brighten someone's corner of the internet.

A simple hug, even a virtual one, can be a reminder that someone cares, that someone sees you, that you're not alone. That's what I hope these virtual hugs can be. A little spark of warmth in someone's day.

Thanks for being here!

Quick Questions (FAQ)

How long does it take for a hug to be sent?

I try my best to schedule hugs within 1–2 days of receiving the request! Sometimes it might be a bit quicker or slightly longer depending on volume.

Can I send a hug anonymously?

Absolutely! The identity of the sender stays anonymous , unless the sender want to reveal it themselves.

Is there a limit to how many hugs I can request?

There's absolutely no limit. Send as much hugs as you want :D

What if the Twitter handle is wrong?

I double-check handles, but mistakes happen! If a hug doesn't seem to go through or targets the wrong account, feel free to DM @hugforiu on Twitter.
